Why use music to relax?

Studies have shown that music greatly improves the moods of those who are experiencing feelings of stress or anxiety. Some studies suggest that in individuals experiencing feelings of anxiety, that music has a stronger effect than even some anti-anxiety medications. Music is an easily accessible and readily available form of help for those seeking relief from stress or anxiety.
